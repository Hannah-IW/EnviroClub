Recycling

Coffee Cup Recycling

We recycle coffee cups as part of the 7/11 Cup Rescue Simply Cups program. We purchase these coffee cup recycling stations from 7/11 and we position them outside a few teacher staffrooms so that the coffee purchased by the staff can be thrown away there instead of it going to landfill. Then once a week these coffee cups are taken up to 7/11 to be recycled and used for future projects.


Pen and Battery Recycling

We have dedicated recycling efforts for pens, batteries, mobile phones and printer cartridges. Positined outside the TAS staffroom are designated recycling station boxes, providing a convenient location for students to responsbily dispose of these items. Once a significant quantity has been collected, the items are transported to our local 7/11 store for recycling. By doing so, we can actively contribute to reducing waste and promoting a more environmentally conscious approach, ensuring that materials which would have otherwise ended up in landfills are given a new lease on life through reuse.
