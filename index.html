About us



The Mosman High School Environment Club meets every Tuesday during lunchtime, dedicated to fostering a greener future and addressing environmental concerns within the school community. We do activities such as volunteering, gardening and participation in school conferences and events. By nurturing a culture of sustainability and initiative, the Environment Club strives to create a more eco-conscious school environment where students are empowered to take meaningful action towards a healthier planet.

What we do

Recycling

The Mosman High School Environment Club actively participates in recycling programs, including the 7/11 coffee cup initiative, as well as collecting and recycling pens, printer cartridges, and batteries. Through these efforts, we contribute to a more sustainable school environment and promote responsible waste management practices.

Read more ->

Gardening

Every Friday afternoon, Environment Club members meet at Memory Park Garden to plant native species and engage in communal gardening. This hands-on activity not only fosters a sense of environmental responsibility but also enriches our community by enhancing green spaces and promoting biodiversity conservation.

Read more ->

Volunteering

The Mosman High School Environment Club engages in diverse volunteering actions such as Clean Up Australia and Wildlife Watch NSC. These activities foster community connections and instil a sense of collective responsibility, empowering members to work towards the greater good and contribute positively to their surroundings.

Events

Chowder Bay

The Environment Club journeyed to Chowder Bay for an enlightening event focused on sustainability. Amidst discussions and tours, they explored seahorse protection and breeding facilities, deepening their understanding of marine conservation and their commitment to safeguarding our oceans for future generations.

Read more ->

School Conference

In August, the Environment Club will host a school conference, inviting students from neighboring schools to witness our eco-initiatives at Mosman High School. Showcasing our environmental endeavors, we aim to inspire collaboration and forge alliances with other schools, united in our mission to effect meaningful change together.

Read more ->

Taronga Zoo

In 2022, the Environment Club engaged in a Trash Hack competition at Taronga Zoo's Green Schools event. Amidst a showcase of diverse environmental initiatives from various schools, we connected with community leaders, fostering collaboration and knowledge exchange. It was an invaluable opportunity to unite, learn, and amplify our impact.
