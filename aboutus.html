About Us

Who are we

The Mosman High School Environment Club is a dedicated group of individuals passionate about improving the environment with local initiatives. We meet every Tuesday during lunchtime where we gather and brainstorm ideas, and plan out projects and contribute to anti-landfill initiatives such as recycling batteries, mobile phones and coffee cups.



Our Goals

Our collective mission within the Mosman High School Environment Club is to actively contribute towards shaping a greener, more sustainable future. We strive to achieve this by creating goals and implementing activities centered around promoting sustainability, preserving natural resources and taking proactive measures to minimise our ecological footprint.
